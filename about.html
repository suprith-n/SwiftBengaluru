About Swift Community

Our Mission

Swift Community is a global network of developers passionate about Apple's Swift programming language. Our mission is to foster collaboration, learning, and innovation within the Swift ecosystem.

What We Do

We organize meetups, workshops, and conferences to help Swift developers connect, learn, and grow. We also provide resources, tutorials, and a platform for sharing knowledge and best practices.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity

    We welcome developers of all backgrounds and skill levels.

  • Innovation

    We encourage creative solutions and pushing the boundaries of what's possible with Swift.

  • Collaboration

    We believe in the power of working together to solve problems and build amazing apps.

  • Education

    We are committed to helping developers learn and master Swift.

Our History

2014

Swift Announced

Apple introduces Swift at WWDC 2014, and our community begins to form.

2015

Swift Goes Open Source

Swift becomes open source, leading to a surge in community involvement.

2016

Swift Community Founded

Our organization is officially established to support the growing Swift ecosystem.

2018

First Global Conference

We host our first international Swift conference, bringing together developers from around the world.

2020

Virtual Community

We transition to virtual events, expanding our reach to developers worldwide.

Today

Growing Strong

Our community continues to grow, with thousands of members across the globe.
